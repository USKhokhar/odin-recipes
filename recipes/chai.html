CHAI (Indian Tea)

Masala Chai is the uncrowned queen of Teas. Popular around the South Asian Peninsula, it is the drink of the people, all the people!

A hot, heavenly drink, made with herbs (and awesomeness).

Here are the ingredients required to make a Chai:

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea Leaves
  • Sugar
  • Ginger
  • Black Pepper
  • Basel Leaves

Steps:

  1. Start boiling water.
  2. When the water is boiled enough, add tea leaves.
  3. Now add sugar.
  4. Add crushed ginger, black pepper, and basel leaves.
  5. Wait till the water is boiled enough. When it is, add milk.
  6. Now wait for the milk to boil.
  7. Your tea is ready yo serve. ENJOY!
